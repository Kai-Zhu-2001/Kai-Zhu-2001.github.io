About

I am a Ph.D. student in Medicinal Chemistry with a strong interest in developing and applying machine learning methods to enhance molecular simulations.

Currently pursuing my Ph.D. at Zhejiang University, School of Pharmacy (2023–2028) under the supervision of Prof. Hou Tingjun, I am also privileged to be a visiting student at the Italian Institute of Technology, working with Prof. Luigi Bonati and Prof. Michele Parrinello.

My research focuses on machine learning-based enhanced sampling methods for molecular simulations. I'm particularly interested in developing computational approaches that can accelerate the exploration of complex molecular systems and rare events, with applications in drug discovery and materials science.

Research Interests

My research focuses on developing machine learning-based enhanced sampling methods for molecular simulations. I'm particularly interested in:

  • Developing novel algorithms that combine machine learning with enhanced sampling techniques
  • Applying these methods to study complex biomolecular systems
  • Improving the efficiency and accuracy of molecular dynamics simulations
  • Exploring applications in drug discovery and materials science
